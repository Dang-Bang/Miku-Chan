Miku-Chan

A Simple Open-Source Discord Bot that Makes your Moderating Job a Lot Easier

Miku-Chan runs off of Discord.JS, an API wrapper made by fans of Discord. Miku-Chan was made by hardcore Discord lovers for hardcore Discord Lovers. So, it includes key features for running your server like purging commands, cleaning the existence of a person off your server, filtering langauge, and more. And, it comes with fun features as well, such as music, lotteries, and voting.

Moderating

With commands like $ban and $erase, people you don't want on your server can stay off and not a trace will be left on.
Betting

Bet Yen, the virtual currency of Miku-Chan, on football, soccer, and more.
Music

Rocking out to that poor SD audio? We've got HD audio streaming clearly through Discord OPUS's Speakers.
Customization

Want to Assign Roles at Join? Want to Give a Donation at Join? Simply a $setjoinrole or $setjoindonation will give your newcomers a reason to stay.
Gaming

Play mini-games like Tic-Tac-Toe and Sudoku with friends. Maybe even put a few dollars on the line?
Time Travel Plugin

Know a certain Pull Request or commit that you still want? You can always use $timetravel to go back to any version. Well, except future builds.
The Clean Web Filtering

Tired of spam bots and other annoying links that give you viruses? A simple $tcw -enable will wipe them out and keep them out until disabled by the person who starts it.
Memes and Jokes

With plenty of memes thanks to KnowYourMemes.com and plenty of jokes from many kids, we've got plenty $jokes and $memes for everyone to have.
