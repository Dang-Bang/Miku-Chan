The Creators

Eager to help the idea behind Miku-Chan, Matthew supported his friend who goes under the psedonym, Miku, to make her a Discord bot. With experience in Python, VB.NET, C#, and C++, it wasn't too tough of a challange. However, Python hosting was a bigger pain than hoped. So, before calling on friends, he switched the main coding language behind the project from Discord.PY to Discord.JS, or from Python to JavaScript.

Since Matthew knew little to no proffesional JavaScript other than simple HTML/JS integrations, he had to call on a trusty friend who's, preferrably, had experience with Discord.JS or bots in JS

Lucas, Matthew's friend, was then called upon to help out with the coding. He accepted the challange, and helped create a hefty amount of the bot and it's integrations with Discord. Lucas, having experience from his "nerdier Minecraft days" had experience in not just JavaScript but other Web Development coding structures that helped jumpstart the speed of Miku-Chan's launch.
